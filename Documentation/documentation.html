I. Abstract

Herbal plant manager website is a comprehensive online resource that provides information on various types of plants, their properties, and their uses. This website is designed to help individuals who are interested in learning about different herbs, plants, and their medicinal or therapeutic benefits.

The website contains a vast database of different types of plants, which can be searched based on their common or scientific name. Each plant entry includes detailed information on the plant's physical characteristics, habitat, growing conditions, and uses. This information can be used by individuals who want to grow these plants at home, use them for cooking or herbal remedies, or simply learn about their unique properties.

Authors: Codreanu Robert & Calinciuc Laura

II. Introduction

Web Gardening is a project that focuses on producing a web application that provides users with suggestions for their house plants and gardens while also allowing them to explore and discover a wide variety of house, tropical, herbal plants, etc.

Authors: Codreanu Robert & Calinciuc Laura

2.1 The application's intended customers

Web Gardening is a web program that provides suggestions and resources to gardeners and plant enthusiasts for caring for their spring plants and encouraging them to expand their plant collection. Our app's target users are curious people in search of a new hobby and plant lovers, who want to just admire plants.

For the ones who want to admire, the app provides a wide range collection of images of plants (FEED), which can be filtered by type, color, etc.

For the ones who want to share, the app provides enhanced tools for importing pictures of plants. Users can create their own collection and manage them online using the application.

Authors: Codreanu Robert & Calinciuc Laura

2.2 Purpose

The app purpose is to raise awareness about plants and the hobby of having/acquiring plants and their benefits for the spiritual and psychologycal health, to have plants more accessible and enjoyable for a wider range of people, and to provide a platform for plant enthusiasts to connect and share their knowledge and experiences.

Authors: Codreanu Robert & Calinciuc Laura

2.3 Vision

This app's vision is to foster an active network of plant enthusiasts who can connect, share their plants and ideas, and learn from one another. By providing a user-friendly design and a wide range of resources, such as plant care manuals.

III. Structure

3.1 Application theme (colors)

Green is a natural and refreshing color that is often associated with growth, health, and nature. Plants are also naturally green, so it makes sense to incorporate this color into a plant-based website. Green is calming and relaxing, which can help visitors feel more connected to nature and the environment. Black, on the other hand, is a bold and sophisticated color that can help create a sense of elegance and sophistication. When paired with green, it can create a strong contrast that can make the green stand out even more. Black can also help to create a sense of depth and dimension on a website, which can make the content more visually appealing and engaging.

Adding white to a green and black color scheme can create a sense of balance and harmony. White is a neutral color that can help to soften the boldness of black and provide a clean and fresh backdrop for the green. White can also help to create a sense of spaciousness and clarity, which can make the website feel more open and inviting.

Incorporating grey into a green, black, and white color scheme for a plant-based website can add a touch of subtlety and sophistication. Grey is a neutral color that can help to balance the boldness of black and provide a sense of depth and texture to the design.

Authors: Codreanu Robert & Calinciuc Laura

3.2 Title

The name "Hema" has several possible meanings and origins, but one of its meanings is "golden" in Sanskrit. This name could be a good fit for a plant-based website because it evokes the idea of something precious, valuable, and natural, like gold or other precious metals.

The name Hema can also have connotations of growth and abundance, which are important themes in the world of plants. Just as a seed can grow into a beautiful and thriving plant, the name Hema suggests the potential for growth, abundance, and prosperity.

Authors: Codreanu Robert & Calinciuc Laura

3.3 Logo

A simple and effective logo for the Hema plant-based website could be the word "Hema" written in a clean and modern font. The letters could be in a bold and striking green color, which would emphasize the website's focus on nature and the environment. To add a touch of elegance and sophistication, the letters could be set against a white or light grey background, which would create a sense of contrast and balance. The word "Hema" could be written in all caps to create a strong and memorable visual impression.

3.4 Home Page

Our website's 'Home' page serves as the central hub for users to access all the different features and pages available on our site. All users can easily navigate through the website using the navigation bar, located at the top of the home page. From here, users can access the 'Help', 'About', and different types of plants pages, allowing them to quickly find the information or products they need. The login/register page can be accessed from the top right corner of the page, as well as under the introduction paragraph. Also, there is a multicriteria search bar, which allows users to find the plants they are looking for and some categories of plants are easily accessible by clicking sugesting pictures. Finally, clicking on the website's logo takes the user back to the home page.

Authors: Codreanu Robert & Calinciuc Laura

3.5 Categories pages (tropical.html, house.html, )

Visitors can access the shop page if they want to purchase spring flowers from our site or even if they just want to look around. Users can then choose the desired product from the list, and to make their search easier, they can filter the products by price (low to high or high to low), by best sellers, or by newest. Users can also use ratings or the name of the flower they wish to filter the products.

IV.The users and their roles in the application

4.1 User

A user can use the application in several ways, such as browsing the available plants in the collection, adding photos in their collection, checking out, and leaving comments.

The user can create an account and save their preferences and personal information. In summary, the user can use the application to browse and review products, access plant information, connect with other gardeners, manage their account.
