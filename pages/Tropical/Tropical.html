TROPICAL PLANTS

Tropical plants are plants that are native to tropical regions around the world. These regions are typically located near the equator and have warm temperatures, high humidity, and abundant rainfall throughout the year. Tropical plants have adapted to these conditions and are typically characterized by their lush foliage, vibrant colors, and exotic blooms.
